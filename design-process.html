ab Design

It's all about communication.

The Design Phase

On day 1 of the project timeline, we will call you to discuss how you want your website to look. Whether you want us to mimic the look and feel of an existing website, or you have a completely original design idea in mind, the floor is yours.

The minute that the phone call disconnects, we're already working on creating the first mock-up prototype in our design software. Once we feel like we've captured your idea, we'll send you a copy that you can review.

If you come across anything in the mock-up that you'd like to change, no worries! We offer FREE EDITS to the design up until 7 days before the end of the project timeline.

The Development Phase

Once you've given us the green light on the design, we get to work developing clean, hand-written HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript source code.

As part of our standard processes and procedures, we always analyze our source code using Google's Lighthouse, a trusted webpage performance analyzer, in order to acheive the best webpage performance possible.

The Delivery Phase

Your website is safe with us, thanks to a big name you can trust. Hosting your website on Netlify's cloud server comes with peace of mind that your data and web traffic is, and always will be, secure.

To summarize:

  • You tell us what you want.

  • We bring your ideas to life in an
    interactive and responsive website.
